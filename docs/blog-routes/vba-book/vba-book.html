Recipe Book for Excel-y Homecooks

Need a free, online recipe book to hold your recipes?

As a homecook and Excel/VBA enthusiast navigating the post-college lifestyle, I realized how useful a recipe book would be. I was playing around with other mediums, but could not find one that I enjoyed that was also for free. Thus, the idea came where I programmed my own recipe book within Excel! Using the file as the book and the sheets as the pages, I added a built-in navigation system, add/delete/edit, and "copy ingredients to shopping list" functionalities via the macro buttons.

Thus, this recipe book was born!
