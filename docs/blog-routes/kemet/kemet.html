Boardgame Battle Visualizer

An Excel/VBA based battle visualizer that quickly determines combat outcomes in Kemet!

For those that have ever played the zone-control board game called Kemet, this visualizer might be useful! Kement is strategic war-game where most elements of the game reward game theory and foresight. I made this visualizer one day to further improve my combat intuition in this game!
